Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of hourly traffic behavior throughout 2023. It explores time-based congestion cycles, weather effects, and event-driven spikes. The purpose is to help decision-makers understand mobility trends and optimize transport planning.

Overall Traffic Volume Trend (Yearly Overview)

This chart presents a full-year traffic time series, showing global peaks, dips, and movement patterns.

Hourly Traffic Time Series

This visualization shows how traffic volume fluctuates hour-by-hour across the entire year. It highlights weekday rush hours, weekend behavior, and natural daily cycles.

Traffic Intensity Heatmap

This heatmap compares hours of the day vs. days of the week to identify high-congestion periods. It helps reveal peak hours, weekend shifts, and consistency in traffic demand.

Weather Impact Analysis

Weather conditions influence travel patterns. This scatter plot compares traffic volumes under Clear, Cloudy, and Rainy weather to show how commuters respond to changing climate conditions.

Event Impact Analysis

Major events (concerts, sports games, holidays) cause notable spikes in traffic. This boxplot visualizes traffic volume differences between event days and normal days.
